2. Cwangcisa idilesi ye-IP ye-IP: nxibelelana noMboneleli weNkonzo ye-Intanethi (i-ISP) kwaye ucele idilesi ye-IP ye-IP yoqhagamshelo lwakho lwe-Intanethi. Oku kuyakuqinisekisa ukuba iwebhusayithi yakho ifikeleleke nokuba idilesi yakho ye-IP itshintsha.


3. Qwalasela i-router yakho: Ngena kwiphaneli yolawulo ye-router yakho kunye ne-Port Port 80 (i-port engagqithanga kwi-IP ye-IP) kwidilesi ye-IP yangaphakathi yeseva yakho. Oku kuya kuvumela ukugcwala kwangaphandle ukufikelela kwiseva yakho yewebhu.


4. Faka kwaye uqwalasele isoftware yeseva: Faka isoftware yomncedisi kumshini weseva wakho kwaye uqwalasele ngokweemfuno zakho, ezinjengokumisela i-misves yakho, izatifikethi ze-SSL, njl.


5. Vavanya iwebhusayithi yakho: Jonga ukuba iwebhusayithi yakho ifikeleleke ngokufaka idilesi yakho ye-IP ye-IP ye-IP kwisikhangeli sewebhu. Ungayivavanya usebenzisa izixhobo kwi-Intanethi njengePingdom okanye Gtmetrix.

8. Jonga kwaye ujonge: Ukubeka iliso rhoqo kwiseva yakho yemicimbi yentsebenzo, izoyikiso zezokhuseleko, kunye nohlaziyo lwesoftware. Gcina isoftware yakho yeseva kunye nezicelo ezenziweyo ukuza kuthi ga ngoku ukuqinisekisa ukusebenza kakuhle.
