2. Gadzirisa postfix: postfix yekugadzirisa mafaera ari mu `/ etc / postfix /`. Iyo huru yekugadzirisa faira ndeye `main.cf`. Iwe unogona kugadzirisa iyi faira kuti uise yako tsamba server maererano nezvaunoda. Mamwe magadzirirwo akajairika iwe ungangoda kusanganisa iyo zita rezita, tsamba dzekuzvidzora, nzvimbo dzemagetsi, nezvimwe.

4 Izvi zvinogona kuitwa uchishandisa iyo `virtal_alias_maps` uye` Virtual_mailbox_mabbox_maps in the postfix yekugadzirisa faira.


5. Chengetedza yako tsamba server: shuwa kuti tsamba yako server yakachengeteka nekumisikidza mireya mitemo, uchishandisa TLS encryption yekupinda uye inobuda mamwe matanho ekuchengetedza akakurudzirwa maseva tsamba.
